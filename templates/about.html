About This Project

Aesthetic Analysis of Hair and Beard Styling for Diverse Face Shapes

Abstract

The styling of human hair and beards plays a significant role in shaping facial appearance, influencing perceived identity, personality, and cultural expression. As one of the most visually prominent features of the human body, hair and beard styles contribute to first impressions by framing facial structures and enhancing or softening key anatomical features.

This project explores the relationship between hairstyle, beard design, and facial morphology, focusing on how intentional grooming choices can harmonize with or modify the visual proportions of the face. By examining a broad range of face shapes—including oval, round, square, rectangular, diamond, and heart-shaped—we highlight styling principles that help achieve aesthetic balance.
